I have interned at companies such as Microsoft, Goldman Sachs, and AT&T, as well as conducted data analysis research at the UW's ICTD lab.

I developed a full-stack webapp feature for an internal finance tool at Goldman Sachs.

I have experience in TypeScript React and Java Springboot, and proficiency in Docker, Kubernetes, Python Pandas and C/C++.
